### NEW SEO-Focused FAQs (Add these for LSI coverage)

#### H3: How much does a custom website cost in Toronto?
Our typical projects range from $5,000 to $15,000 depending on scope, features, and complexity. We offer custom quotes based on your specific business needs and goals. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed estimate.

#### H3: Do you offer e-commerce website design?
Yes, we specialize in e-commerce development using platforms like Shopify and WooCommerce. We build online stores optimized for mobile shopping, secure checkout, and conversion.

#### H3: Can you redesign my existing website?
Absolutely. We offer website redesign services to modernize outdated sites with mobile-first, accessible design. We'll preserve your existing content while improving user experience, performance, and SEO.

#### H3: Do you work with businesses outside Toronto?
Yes. While we're based in Toronto, we serve clients across the Greater Toronto Area including North York, Scarborough, Etobicoke, Mississauga, Markham, Vaughan, Brampton, and Richmond Hill. We also work with businesses throughout Ontario and across Canada.

#### H3: Do your websites meet accessibility standards?
Yes. We build websites that follow WCAG compliance guidelines to ensure accessibility for all users. This includes proper heading structure, alt text for images, keyboard navigation, and screen reader compatibility.

#### H3: What's the difference between UI and UX design?
UI (user interface) design focuses on the visual elements—colors, typography, buttons. UX (user experience) design focuses on how users interact with your site—navigation, flow, usability. We combine both to create websites that look great and function seamlessly.
